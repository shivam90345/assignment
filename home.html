With stunning views of the ocean, Example Cafe offers the perfect environment to unwind and recharge the batteries.

Our menu offers a wide range of breakfasts,
brunches and lunches, including a range of
vegetarian options.

Whether you sip on a fresh, hot coffee or a cooling
smoothie, you never need to feel rushed - relax with
friends or just watch the world go by.
